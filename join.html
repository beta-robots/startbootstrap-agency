Equilibrium poses of dropped objects

Universal detector

Today's robots can embark many different types of cameras. The goal of this intership is to wrap our several detection ROS packages to build a single detector package that allows the use of many types of cameras and detection techniques. The result should be camera-independant and allow the rapid and efficient addition of new detectors. This position involves intensive coding, apply if you feel comfortable with it.
Level: Engineering/Computer Science/Mathematics Masters or Graduate.
Duration: 6 months
Apply by sending a mail to jobs@beta-robots.com

Noisy towns
